# Refund Policy

## Overview

Customers may request a full refund within 30 days of purchase. Refunds are processed to the original payment method within 5–10 business days.

## Eligibility

- Product must be unused or in original condition
- Proof of purchase required
- Subscription services cancelled before renewal date receive a prorated refund

## Non-refundable Items

- Digital downloads after 14 days
- Custom or personalized products
- Gift cards

## Contact

For refund requests, email support@example.com with your order number.
